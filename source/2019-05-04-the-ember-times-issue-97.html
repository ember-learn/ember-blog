They have recently [announced](https://twitter.com/EmberFest/status/1125441719921717251) that the CFP is **now open** with a deadline at August 1, 2019. EmberFest is looking for a wide range of talks, from in-depth technical talks to broader talks covering other aspects of software development. While Ember-related is best, EmberFest is open to considering talks that cover more general software / tech related topics that would be of interest to Ember developers.
